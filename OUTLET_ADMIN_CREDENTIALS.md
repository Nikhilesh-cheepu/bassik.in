## Notes

- All outlet admins are **hardcoded** in the system (not stored in database)
- Outlet admins can ONLY:
  - View and edit their own venue details
  - Upload/delete cover photos and gallery images for their venue
  - Manage menus for their venue
  - View bookings for their venue only
  - Update booking status for their venue only
- Outlet admins CANNOT:
  - Access other outlets
  - View bookings from other outlets
  - Edit other outlet data
  - Manage other admins
- Cover photos can now be uploaded in **any aspect ratio** (16:9 restriction removed)
- All changes made by outlet admins are saved to the database and visible to main admin and users
